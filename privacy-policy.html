By using our service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information you provide when using our WiFi services. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.
